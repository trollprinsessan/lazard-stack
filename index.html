The energy transition is a power transition. Climate policy is industrial policy. Europe gets one chance to rewire itself for 2040, or it imports the future from someone else.

Olivia Lazard sets out the diagnosis. Twelve domains where Europe's status quo is failing, and the twelve moves that replace it. Three layers, bottom to top, the foundations, the engine room, the money.

What is the Electro Union?

A 2040 Europe organised around electrification, of industry, defence, capital and politics, instead of around imported energy and exported intellectual property. A peace project that meets the stakes of the future, built in partnership with other middle powers. Also an open letter from Norrsken Foundation.

Who is Olivia Lazard?

Founder of Transition Intelligence, Berggruen Institute Fellow, Director of Peace in Design Consulting.

Why a stack and not a list?

Because climate policy is industrial policy is regenerative policy. A stack has interdependent layers that need to be designed for prolific innovation and cross-fertilisation at and across every layer. Pick recommendations at the expense of others and the whole architecture stops compounding.

What happens if Europe doesn't do this?

Europe becomes a museum with questionable public transport.

Is this a joke?

No.
